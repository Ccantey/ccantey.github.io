About Madison CrimeViz

The Data

This dataset was acquired from the Madison Police Department. The time frame of this data is from January, 2012 to October, 2012. It contains all incidence of gun-related crime throughout that time period. You can toggle the crimeviz to view these individual point, crime scene locations. This information falls under the public domain, however, it is not my intention to reveal, or bring to attention, any victim identity information.

Please respect the privacy of the victims in these cases.
